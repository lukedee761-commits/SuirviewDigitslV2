02 — What she does

A receptionist who never sleeps, never rushes, never guesses.

Answers every call, day or night

No voicemail, no hold music, no ringing out. Ava picks up instantly — at 3pm on a Tuesday or 9pm on a Sunday — so the customer who’d have rung someone else stays yours.

Books straight into your calendar

She checks what’s free, offers the caller a time, and books it in — then reads the details back so nothing’s wrong.

Answers your common questions

Opening hours, where you’re parked, what you do, what to bring — from a knowledge base you approve, in your words.

Reschedules and cancels

Not just new bookings — she moves and cancels appointments too, so your calendar stays honest without you touching it.

She never guesses — and that’s the point

If something isn’t in the knowledge base you signed off, Ava won’t invent an answer. She says she’ll have someone come back to you and takes a message. She won’t quote a price she can’t stand over, and she won’t give advice she isn’t qualified to give. A receptionist who makes things up is worse than no receptionist at all.

Takes messages and callbacks

Name, number, and what it’s about — read back and confirmed, waiting for you when you’re free.

07 — FAQ

Asked before you ask.

What if she doesn’t know the answer?

She never guesses. If it isn’t in the knowledge base you approved, she says she’ll have someone come back to you and takes a message. She won’t quote a price she can’t stand over, and she won’t give advice she isn’t qualified to give.

Can callers still get a human?

Always. If someone asks for a person, sounds frustrated or urgent, or hits any scenario you pick, Ava passes the call straight through to you.

Will she sound like a robot?

She speaks in full sentences, at a normal pace, and says numbers the way people actually say them. The honest test isn’t a sample we picked, though — it’s your own demo agent. We’ll put one on a number within 24 hours so you can ring it yourself and try to catch her out.

Do I need a new phone number?

No. You keep the number that’s on your van, your cards and your Google listing — you just forward it. All the time, or only after hours and when the line is busy. It’s your switch.

What happens if I go over 500 minutes?

It’s €0.30 a minute after that, billed on what you actually used — no bill shock, no surprise tier. If you’re regularly over, we’ll talk about a plan that suits you rather than let it run.

How fast can she really be live?

You’ll be ringing your own demo agent within 24 hours of our first call. She goes live on your real number in around 72 hours — and only once you’ve said you’re happy.
